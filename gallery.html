Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played between two teams of five players each. The objective is to score by shooting a ball through the opposing team's hoop. It is popular worldwide, particularly in the U.S. and China.

Location: United States

Football

Football, also known as soccer in some countries, is a team sport where two teams of eleven players aim to score by kicking a ball into the opposing goal. It is the most popular sport globally.

Location: Worldwide

Swimming

Swimming is a sport and recreational activity that involves moving through water using arms and legs. It is an Olympic sport, and its forms include freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, and butterfly.

Location: Worldwide

Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is particularly popular in South Asia, Australia, and England. The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets.

Location: India, Australia, England
