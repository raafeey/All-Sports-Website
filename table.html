Overview of Popular Sports

Sport Equipment Rules
Basketball Basketball, hoop, court Score by shooting the ball into the opposing team’s hoop. The team with the most points wins.
Football Football, goalposts, cleats Teams of 11 try to score by kicking the ball into the opposing team’s goal. Most goals win.
Swimming Swimsuit, goggles, swimming pool Swim laps in a pool. First to finish wins the race in various styles: freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, and butterfly.
Cricket Bat, ball, wickets, gloves Score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets. The team with the most runs wins.
Tennis Tennis racket, ball, tennis court Two or four players hit the ball over a net. Points are won when the opponent fails to return the ball within the bounds.
Volleyball Volleyball, net, court Two teams hit the ball over the net. A team scores a point when the ball lands in the opposing team’s court.
